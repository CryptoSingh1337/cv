  • Finding Genes From DNA

    This project I made during my Course on Coursera. It basically find all the Genes from DNA
    with "ATG" StartCodon and "TAA", "TAG", "TGA" StopCodon at the end. It also have some features like :

    1. Counting No. of Genes in that DNA.
    2. Calculating the CG Ratio.
    3. Count No. of "CTG" strand in DNA.
    4. Calculating the Length of Longest Gene.
    5. Counting all the Genes which have length > 60 and having CG Ratio > 0.35.
    6. Displaying all the Genes.

    See project..
